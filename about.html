About

Our innovative system combines the power of Flask, MoviePy, OpenCV, and the Whisper model to automate video captioning and translation. It seamlessly processes videos in real-time, extracting key features with MoviePy and generating highly accurate captions using the Whisper model. These captions are then translated into multiple languages, enhancing accessibility for a global audience. Our user-friendly interface, powered by Flask, ensures easy deployment and smooth operation, making video captioning and translation more efficient and accessible than ever before.
